# Possum Patrol — Services & Pricing

*Last updated by Marshall, sometime in 2022. Skye has been winging it since.*

Prices are "what Marshall usually charges." Real prices have varied wildly based on Marshall's mood, the customer's vibe, and whether it's raining.

- Sanitization after removal: +$100 – $300

- Travel surcharge (outside 30-mile radius): +$50 – $125

## Marshall's Pricing Philosophy (in his own words)

> "If they're nice on the phone, fair price. If they're rude, top of the range. If they're a church or a teacher, give 'em $25 off. If they argue about the bill before I've even started, decline the job. Life's too short."

> "Never quote a snake removal under the phone unless you've seen pictures. People can't tell a king snake from a copperhead and you'll regret it."

> "Commercial accounts pay on time. Always do a written quote. Always."
